1. What types of cakes do you offer? We offer a wide variety of cakes, including classic flavors like chocolate and vanilla, as well as specialty options like red velvet, carrot cake, and fruit-filled cakes. 2. How do I place an order? Ordering a cake is simple! Just browse our website, select the cake you like, choose the size, flavor. Proceed to order, where you can provide delivery details and make the payment. 3. Can I customize my cake? Absolutely! We offer customization options for many of our cakes. You can choose the frosting color, add a personalized message. 4. How much notice do you need for an order? To ensure the best quality, we recommend placing your order at least 4 hours in advance using our Angular Material date picker. For custom or large orders, more notice may be required. However, we do have a selection of "Ready to Order" cakes that can be purchased with shorter notice. 5. Do you offer delivery? Yes, we provide delivery services within our designated areas. Delivery fees may vary based on distance. During the checkout process, you can enter your location using the Angular Material auto-complete feature to check if we deliver to your area. 6. Can I pick up my order instead of having it delivered? Certainly! You can "Pickup" option during checkout.
